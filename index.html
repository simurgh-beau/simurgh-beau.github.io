Executive Summary

[cite_start]"The protocol is remarkably efficient, capable of distilling the views of a population of 100 million participants down to a core group of 100 in as few as six weeks[cite: 12]. [cite_start]This tool is designed not only to bridge internal divisions within a populace but also to provide a clear, data-driven barometer of the people's true will, empowering them to move forward with unity and a shared sense of purpose[cite: 13]."

This protocol establishes a secure, anonymous, and multi-stage deliberation process where citizens can discuss and vote on pressing national issues[cite: 10]. [cite_start]Through a meritocratic filtration system, the most resonant and well-reasoned ideas rise to the top, culminating in a national consensus that is transparent and visible to all participants[cite: 11].
